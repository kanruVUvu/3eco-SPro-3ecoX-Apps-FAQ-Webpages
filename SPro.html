How to request a Manual Ride?

  • ‌Manual Ride / Delivery is the Tab where the Requests are being taken manually by the SPro while driving their Vehicle wherein the SPro can start their Ride once they pickup a Customer from a pickup point.
    • ‌ ‌
  • If the SPro wants to end the Ride in Manual Ride / Delivery Tab he can do so by clicking the End Ride Button .
  • ‌Once the Manual Ride has been completed the corresponding Sales Details is being entered.
  • And on click of Submit Button , The Ride has been completely finished .

How to request a Manual Delivery?

  • ‌Manual Ride / Delivery is the Tab where the Requests are being taken manually by the SPro while driving their Vehicle wherein the SPro can start their Delivery once they pickup the Parcel from a pickup point.
    • ‌ ‌
  • If the SPro has delivered the Parcel at the Destination Point he can end the Delivery in Manual Ride / Delivery Tab by clicking the End Ride Button .
  • Once the Manual Delivery has been completed the corresponding Sales Details is being entered
  • And on click of Submit Button , The Delivery has been completely finished .

How do I access Reports for Ride Bookings?

  • Ride / Delivery History is a tab which gives complete information about Completed and Cancelled Manual / Normal Rides .
    • ‌ ‌
  • Ride / Delivery Summary Tab gives complete information about All the Manual/Normal Rides , Submitted Manual/Normal Rides and Pending Manual/Normal Rides .

How do I access Reports for Delivery Bookings?

  • Ride / Delivery History is a tab which gives complete information about Completed and Cancelled Manual/Normal Deliveries .
    • ‌ ‌
  • Ride / Delivery Summary Tab gives complete information about All the Manual/Normal Deliveries , Submitted Manual/Normal Deliveries and Pending Manual/Normal Deliveries .

Can I give Rating and Comments about my Customer in SPro App after Completion of a Ride?

Yes, Drivers can share the feedback of Customer and their RIDE experience in scale of 1-5 . Drivers can also write their Comments about their Customer in the SPro App after Completion of Ride.

Can I give Rating and Comments about my Customer in SPro App after Completion of a Delivery?

Yes, Drivers can share the feedback of Customer and their DELIVERY experience in scale of 1-5 . Drivers can also write their Comments about their Customer in the SPro App after Completion of Delivery.

Can I cancel a Upcoming Ride Request after Booking Confirmation?

Yes. To cancel the Upcoming Ride Request after Booking Confirmation in the SPro App please follow the instructions below:Upcoming Ride / Delivery > Cancel Delivery Button to be clicked which is present in Ride Order

Can I cancel a Upcoming Delivery Request after Booking Confirmation?

Yes. To cancel the Upcoming Delivery Request after Booking Confirmation in the SPro App please follow the instructions below:Upcoming Ride / Delivery > Cancel Delivery Button to be clicked which is present in Delivery Order
