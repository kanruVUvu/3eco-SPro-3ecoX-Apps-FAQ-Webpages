How‌ ‌to‌ ‌request‌ ‌a‌n Instant ‌ride?‌

To‌ ‌request‌ ‌a‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌follow‌ ‌the‌ ‌next‌ ‌steps:‌

  • After‌ ‌opening‌ ‌the‌ ‌Application‌ ‌tap‌ ‌“choose‌ ‌pickup‌ ‌location”,‌ ‌the‌ ‌app‌ ‌will‌ ‌set‌ ‌your‌ ‌pickup‌ ‌location‌ ‌via‌ ‌GPS‌ ‌
    • ‌ ‌
  • If‌ ‌you‌ ‌need‌ ‌to‌ ‌change‌ ‌the‌ ‌location,‌ ‌type‌ ‌in‌ ‌your‌ ‌exact‌ ‌address.‌ ‌You‌ ‌can‌ ‌also‌ ‌input‌ ‌your‌ ‌destination‌ ‌there
  • Select‌ ‌whether‌ ‌the‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌is‌ ‌Private‌ ‌or‌ ‌Shared‌
  • In‌ ‌the‌ ‌next‌ ‌Step‌ ‌confirm‌ ‌your‌ ‌Payment‌ ‌Option‌ ‌for‌ ‌the‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌and‌ ‌Confirm‌ ‌the‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌Booking.‌ ‌
  • If‌ ‌you’re‌ ‌getting‌ ‌message‌ ‌“No‌ ‌Rides ‌available”,‌ ‌please‌ ‌wait‌ ‌for‌ ‌a‌ ‌couple‌ ‌of‌ ‌minutes‌ ‌and‌ ‌try‌ ‌to‌ ‌request‌ ‌again.

How‌ ‌are‌ ‌the‌ ‌tariffs‌ ‌calculated‌ ‌for‌ ‌Ride?

3eco‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌fare‌ ‌is‌ ‌calculated‌ ‌as‌ ‌a‌ ‌sum‌ ‌of‌ ‌Minimum‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌(‌ ‌Base‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌)‌ ‌,‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌per‌ ‌Km‌ ‌,‌ ‌Waiting‌ ‌Time‌ ‌(‌ ‌per‌ ‌minute‌ ‌),‌ ‌Cancellation‌ ‌Charge‌ ‌and‌ ‌Tax‌ ‌.‌ ‌For‌ ‌Example‌ ‌in‌ Doddaballapura‌ ‌our‌ ‌3-Seater‌ ‌3ev‌ ‌Vehicle‌ ‌has‌ ‌the‌ ‌following‌ ‌Tariff‌ ‌for‌ ‌Private‌ ‌Ride‌ ‌

  • ‌Minimum‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌(‌ ‌Base‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌)‌ ‌-‌ ‌25‌ ‌Rupees‌ ‌for 1.9 Kms ‌
    • ‌ ‌
  • Ride‌ ‌Fare‌ ‌per‌ ‌Km‌ ‌-‌ ‌13 ‌Rupees‌
  • ‌Waiting‌ ‌Time‌ ‌(‌ ‌Per‌ ‌minute‌ ‌)‌ ‌-‌ ‌1‌ ‌Rupee‌ ‌
  • ‌Cancellation‌ ‌Charge‌ ‌-‌ ‌20‌ ‌Rupees‌ ‌
  • Tax‌ ‌-‌ ‌5%‌ ‌

Customers‌ ‌can‌ ‌also‌ ‌view‌ ‌the‌ ‌Tariff‌ ‌Information‌ ‌for‌ ‌Rides‌ ‌in‌ ‌Rate‌ ‌Card‌ ‌Tab‌ ‌of‌ ‌3eco‌ ‌Customer‌ ‌Application‌ .‌

    ‌
  • Customer‌ ‌has‌ ‌to‌ ‌enter‌ ‌the‌ ‌Pick‌ ‌up‌ ‌Address‌ ‌for‌ ‌the‌ ‌parcel.‌ ‌This‌ ‌data‌ ‌can‌ ‌either‌ ‌be‌ ‌fetched‌ ‌automatically‌ ‌from‌ ‌mobile‌ ‌GPS‌ ‌or‌ ‌can‌ ‌be‌ ‌entered‌ ‌manually.‌ ‌ ‌ ‌

  • ‌‌After‌ ‌that‌ ‌we‌ ‌are‌ ‌Confirming‌ ‌Delivery‌ ‌and‌ ‌Initiating‌ ‌our‌ ‌Payment‌ ‌for‌ ‌the‌ ‌Confirmed‌ ‌Delivery‌ ‌.
  • If‌ ‌you’re‌ ‌getting‌ ‌message‌ ‌“No‌ ‌Deliveries ‌available”,‌ ‌please‌ ‌wait‌ ‌for‌ ‌a‌ ‌couple‌ ‌of‌ ‌minutes‌ ‌and‌ ‌try‌ ‌to‌ ‌request‌ ‌again. ‌

Difference between Private Ride and Shared Ride?

  • ‌‌Private Ride is a Ride wherein you can book the Vehicle to yourself.
    • ‌ ‌
  • Shared Ride is a Ride wherein you are booking the Vehicle which is shared by 1 or 2 people
  • In a Private Ride , the Ride Fare is more .
  • ‌‌In a Shared Ride , the Ride Fare is less .

Why there might be varying prices for similar trips ?

There might be varying prices for similar trips in 3eco App because of the dynamic price boost, which varies according to the demand and supply at the moment.
